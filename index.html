Why Not Transfi?

Transfi Blockchain is among the first, or If not the first Blockchain company concerned and focused on improving the problems faced by the transportation sector on a global scale. We are concerned with providing effective solution for the fast and reliable payment of transport services and also using Transfi Blockchain to smoothen the supply chain between stakeholders of the transport sector.

Faster, cheaper, easier payments

Making payments with Transfi Token means cutting the transaction process short. That means low fees and near instant transactions. Making a payment becomes as easy as scanning a QR code.

Secure and Trackable transactions

Payments are secured with the introduction of end to end transactions (P2P) and are stored on the blockchain network. Transactions and products are trackable which creates assurance on saftey of money and goods ( frieght businesses)

Zero Market Volatility

Transfi is a payment option to cover for all freight transactions meaning Market volatility and decisions are determined by the users of the token. Price increase and decrease is determined by users of transfi services
